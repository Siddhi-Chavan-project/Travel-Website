Discover Maldives

The Maldives, a tropical paradise of pristine beaches, crystal-clear waters, and luxurious overwater bungalows. Perfect for honeymooners, divers, and sun-seekers!

Top Places to Visit in Maldives

Alimatha Island

Alimatha offers a perfect blend of soft white sands, vibrant coral reefs, and thrilling water activities like snorkeling, diving, and kayaking.

Kuredu Island Resort

Known for its luxurious amenities, vibrant nightlife, and stunning beaches, Kuredu offers a premium Maldives experience.

Grand Friday Mosque

Located in Malé, this beautiful mosque with its golden dome is one of the largest in the Maldives and showcases stunning Islamic architecture.

Best Time to Visit

November to April offers dry weather, perfect for beach activities and exploring islands.

Top Activities

  • Scuba diving & snorkeling
  • Dolphin & whale watching
  • Underwater dining experiences
  • Sunset cruises & private island picnics

Travel Tips

  • Use speedboats or seaplanes for island transfers
  • Carry reef-safe sunscreen
  • Respect local customs and dress modestly in public islands
